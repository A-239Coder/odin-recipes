Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

Description

Quick and simple macaroni and cheese recipe with a creamy cheese sauce and crisp bake

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 all purpose flower
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Milk
  • Cheese of your choosing cheddar or parmesan recommended

Steps

  1. Boil Macaroni in a pot of boiling water until al dente
  2. Making the roux. start by melting the butter in a saucepan over medium heat add flour and salt and pepper to taste and stir unitl smooth. then slowly pour in milk unitl the mixture is smooth and bubbling. DO NOT BURN THE MILK
  3. Add the cheese to the roux until fully encorporated once pasta is cooked drain and then stir into the roux unitl coated
