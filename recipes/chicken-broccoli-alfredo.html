Chicken and Broccoli Alfredo Recipe

Description

A quick and easy recipe that the whole family will love. the creamy sauce creates a warm comfort dish whilst also remaining healthy thanks to the chicken and broccoli

Ingredients

  • fettuccine
  • 2 cups fresh broccoli
  • 1/4 cup Italian dressing
  • chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 2/3 cups milk
  • Philadelphia cream cheese
  • grated parmesan cheese
  • dried basil leaves

Steps

  1. Cook pasta as stated on the packaging, adding the broccoli to the boiling water for the last 2 minutes of the pasta cooking time and then drain mixture
  2. Meanwhile, heat dressing in a large non stcik skillet on medium-high heat. add chicken and cook until no longer pink in the center, stirring occasionally, about 5 to 7 minutes
  3. Stir in milk, cream cheese, Parmesan cheese and the basil. bring to boil, stirring constantly. Cook until sauce is well blended and heated through, about 1 to 2 minutes
  4. Add chicken mixture to pasta mixture; mix light
