Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of the most popular Italian dishes world wide. it is comprised of sheets of pasta alternating with ragu (ground meats and tomato sauce). and topped with a bechamel sauce and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage
  • Lean ground beef
  • White onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
